Low-temperature measurement platforms

Our experiments rely on state-of-the-art cryogenic infrastructure enabling electronic transport measurements at ultra-low temperatures and high magnetic fields.

Dilution refrigerator equipped with a 16 T magnet and base temperature of 10 mK.

Current systems

We operate dilution refrigerators providing base temperatures down to 10 mK and magnetic fields up to 16 T, allowing precise investigations of quantum transport in low-dimensional systems.
