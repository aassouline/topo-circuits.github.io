# Topological Circuit Group

**Build eletrical circuits based on topological quantum matter**

Welcome to our group website ! Interested in low-dimensional electrical circuits where correlations and topology play a key role in stabilizing novel quantum phenomena ? Contact us ! We develop and study controlled platforms to realize, manipulate, and detect novel **excitations** in **topological phases of matter**, with a focus on fundamental physics and potential applications in topologial quantum computing.

---

## 🔬 Research Highlights

### AnyonBox (ERC Project)
A platform to confine, control, and braid single anyon excitations in ultra-clean graphene systems.

![AnyonBox logo](/assets/images/anyonbox_logo.png)

We investigate:
- Single anyon manipulation and exchange statistics
- Topological protection and decoherence

- ### Sound-driven 2D quantum circuits
We study electronic transport via sound waves. 

We investigate:
- Sound-driven electron transport in topological states
- Electron flying qubit
