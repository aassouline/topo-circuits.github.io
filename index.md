# Topological Circuit Group

**Build eletrical circuits based on topological quantum matter**

Welcome to the Topological Circuit research group. We develop and study controlled platforms to realize, manipulate, and detect novel **excitations** in **topological phases of matter**, with applications to quantum information and fundamental physics.

---

## 🔬 Research Highlights

### AnyonBox (ERC Project)
A platform to confine, control, and braid emergent anyonic excitations in engineered quantum systems.

![AnyonBox logo](/assets/images/anyonbox_logo.png)

We investigate:
- Controlled braiding and exchange statistics
- Topological protection and decoherence
