Electrical circuits based on van der Waals heterostructures

We develop electronic devices based on van der Waals assembly of two-dimensional materials, enabling the realization of high-quality quantum circuits with exceptional electronic properties.

Cleanroom facilities

Our work benefits from the cleanroom facilities of the Néel Institute, which include optical lithography, electron-beam lithography (80 kV), reactive ion etching (RIE), and multiple metal deposition systems (Plassys).
